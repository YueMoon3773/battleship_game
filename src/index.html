How to play

1. Place Your Fleet

Arrange your fleet on the grid. Ships can be placed horizontally or vertically, without overlapping.

2. Start the Battle

On your turn, choose an enemy grid coordinate (e.g., B4) and tap on the enemy grid to fire at a position.

3. Track Hits and Misses

If your shot hits a ship, it is marked in red, otherwise misses are marked in blue.

4. Sink All Opponent Ships to Win

The first to sink all ships wins the game!
